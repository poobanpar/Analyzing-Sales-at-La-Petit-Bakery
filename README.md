 2. Month of the year

Analyzing Product Seasonality is important to efficiently plan advertising, promotions, and manufacturing decisions and investments. Here we can see that smoothie sales spike upward in the summer, but sales of other products exhibit little seasonality.Given the strong seasonality and corresponding uptick in smoothie sales, the bakery can probably “trim” advertising and promotions expenditures for smoothies between April and August. On the other hand, to match the increased demand for smoothies, the bakery may want to guarantee the availability and delivery of the ingredients needed for making its smoothies. Similarly, if the increased demand places stress on the bakery’s capability to serve its customers, it may consider hiring extra workers during the summer months.
